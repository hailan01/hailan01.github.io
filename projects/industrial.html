Trajectory Data Infrastructure

VRE: A Versatile, Robust, and Economical Trajectory Data System (VLDB'22 Industrial)

Hai Lan, Jiong Xie, Zhifeng Bao, Feifei Li, Wei Tian, Fang Wang, Sheng Wang, Ailin Zhang

VRE is a production-scale trajectory data system deployed in Alibaba Cloud's spatiotemporal database engine, serving real workloads over vehicle, vessel, and aviation data. The system is designed for the three properties named in its title — versatility across diverse trajectory data types and query patterns, robustness under production-grade conditions, and economical operation at the scale required by a cloud database product.
