Hai Lan

Postdoc Research Fellow @ The University of Queensland

I am a Postdoc Research Fellow at The University of Queensland. I received my PhD degree in 2025 from the School of Computing at RMIT University, under the supervision of Prof. Zhifeng Bao and Prof. Shane Culpepper.

Prior to that, I obtained my Bachelor's (2017) and Master's (2020) degrees from Wuhan University, supervised by A/Prof. Yuwei Peng.

My research interests lie in:

  • Query optimization
  • Indexing techniques in database systems
  • Applying learning methods to DB problems
  • Trajectory processing and management
